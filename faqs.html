Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Do you have any products that are Gluten-Free?

A: Many of our products can be made gluten-free. Just write gluten-free in the special request box on your order form. If a specific product cannot be made gluten-free, we will let you know.

Q: What should I do if there is a problem with my order or product?

A: Please contact us, and we will be more than happy to help resolve the situation.

Q: Are your products organic?

A: Products labeled "Organic" are organic and we are actively working on expanding our organic selection!

Q: What payment methods do you accept on your site?

A: We accept MasterCard, Visa, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: Unfortunately, we do not offer returns. However, if there is something wrong with your order or products, we would be happy to do full or partial reorder/refund for you.

Q: Can I cancel my order once placed?

A: We unfortunately are unable to cancel orders that are processed or have been fulfilled.

Q: Do you deliver?

A: Yes! Just check off "deliver" in your order form, and your order will come straight to your door!
